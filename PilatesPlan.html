What is pilates?

Pilates is a low-impact form of exercise that emphasizes controlled movement, core strength, and overall body awareness. It combines elements of physical rehabilitation, breathwork, and precise technique to create a full-body conditioning system. At its core, Pilates focuses on strengthening the deep muscles of the abdomen, back, and pelvis—often referred to as the “powerhouse”—which helps improve posture, balance, and stability.
